Would You Like to Support is an application made in my free time during COVID lockdown. I was tired of replaying my favorite podcasts just to hear the ad break, going to content pages to find the creators affiliations, or using mobile browsers while swapping back and forth between social media profiles.

If you feel like this application has earned it, please consider donating so I can continue improving the user experience or, at least, buy my dog a nice treat!
